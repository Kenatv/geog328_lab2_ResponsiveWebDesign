Foods Are Our Puppies


Corgis are active and energetic dogs that require a well-balanced diet to sustain their energy levels and overall health. The ideal diet for a Corgi should include high-quality proteins, essential fats, easily digestible carbohydrates, and a balanced mix of vitamins and minerals. Proper nutrition is crucial for supporting their physical activity and preventing health issues.

Here are some popular and nutritious options for your furry friend:

  • Royal Canin Corgi Adult Dry Dog Food — specially formulated for Corgis, supports joint health and a healthy weight.
  • Hill’s Science Diet Adult Small Bites — made with real chicken, easy to chew, and rich in omega-6 fatty acids for skin and coat care.
  • Blue Buffalo Life Protection Formula — contains whole grains, vegetables, and fruits with no artificial preservatives.
  • Wellness CORE Small Breed — grain-free, high-protein recipe to maintain energy and lean muscle mass.
  • Merrick Lil’ Plates Small Breed Recipe — designed for small dogs, includes glucosamine and chondroitin for joint support.
  • Purina Pro Plan Small Breed Shredded Blend — a mix of crunchy kibble and tender chicken bites packed with antioxidants.

Always consult your vet before changing your corgi’s diet. Portion control is key — Corgis can gain weight quickly due to their small frame!
